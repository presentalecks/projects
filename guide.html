1. How to Setup Two Factor Authentication
  2. 1. Sign into your gmail account at gmail.com and with your cursor, make your way to the colored icon with your initials located at the top right corner of the webpage. Click on it then click on the button that says "Google Account"
  4. 2. You're now located in your Google Account page where you can make changes to different settings. In the sidebar on the left of the page, click on "Security".
  6. 3. In the Security tab, scroll down until you see a section titled "Signing in to Google". Click on 2 Step Verification in the list of settings bellow.
  8. 4. You are now shown a page explaining the security benefits that 2 Step Verification offers. Click on the "Get Started" button located at the bottom right corner to proceed.
  10. 5. The next tab will prompt you to enter your phone number as the number that Google sends a verification code to. You also need to choose whether you want the code sent to you through an automated phone call or text message. Click the "Next" button on the bottom right corner to proceed.
  12. 6. Google will send you a code that will need to entered to enable 2 Step Verification. Enter the code, Click "Next" and you're finished!
  14. 7. The next page shows different options for 2 Step Verification like Google Prompt, which lets you verify with the click of a button, or Google authenticator, which provides access codes even when you're offline.
